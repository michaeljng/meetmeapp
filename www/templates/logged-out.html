What is QuiikMeet?

QuiikMeet is a realtime friend finding service that allows you to search for nearby people and do something together.

Have a break? Meet someone new over a cup of coffee! QuiikMeet fills gaps in your schedule with social interaction.

Meet someone new today!

How to Use It
Create a post.
Send an invitation to someone.
Chat with them and decide where to go.

Authenticate with Facebook.
